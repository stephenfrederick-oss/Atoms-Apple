Variations

Shades and tints give your source color the full range it needs to work across a real UI. Shades blend toward black for depth; tints blend toward white for subtle fills. Every step is labeled 0% to 100%.

Harmonies

Color harmony combines colors in ways that are visually pleasing, based on hue relationships on the color wheel — creating balance, contrast, or cohesion in your palette.

Accessibility Test

WCAG contrast requirements ensure text is readable for all users. A ratio of 4.5:1 is required for normal text at AA level, 3:1 for large text, and 7:1 for AAA.
